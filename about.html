More About Me

I graduated in the Spring of 2022 from the University of Montana with a degree in Psychology. Go Griz! While I was pursuing my psychology degree I was also a part of the University of Montana’s Women’s Griz Golf Team. As a student-athlete, I learned many skills that translate into the professional world. The biggest skill I learned was how important time management was. Between the early morning wake-ups for workouts, a full day of school, and then golf practice I got really good at managing school, golf, and the social aspects of college.

Once I graduated, I got an honors certificate from Nucamp Coding Bootcamp in backend web development and started an internship with Danica’s Custom Bakes. There I learned how to adapt. I learned new coding languages, libraries, databases, and frameworks quickly in order to start contributing to the new updates to the website. Once I finished the internship, I started part-time work with my father’s business in laser engraving. I am currently working on a website to create our online presence. I am also constantly learning about sales and marketing through new certifications from HubSpot in order to contribute to the growth and success of our local business!

A little more about me…

Lately, some of my bigger hobbies have been sewing and fashion in general, gardening, and line dancing. I love learning new languages and building my knowledge and toolsets. When it’s nice and sunny out, playing some golf or tennis is one of my favorite things to do. I grew up around aviation my whole life and have grown interested in the aviaion industry. I am big advocate of bettering myself and my work, there is always room for growth and I like to keep challenging myself in all aspects of my life.
